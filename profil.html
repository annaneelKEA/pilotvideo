The Idea

Who is Uffe?

The whole idea behind the production came when I was tasked with filming a person with a passion. Immediately, I thought of Uffe as he is the coolest person I know. The video was partly filmed at his residence and in the Brøndby Forest.

The video production:

In the video, we focused on evoking emotions in the viewers and achieving that by appealing to pathos through the interview and showcasing some film clips that really captured the joy in his passion. The video was filmed using a mix of a handheld camera and a moving camera.
